Letride Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Letride, a ride-sharing app for the Pi Network community. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: Real-time location to facilitate ride matching and navigation.
  • Transaction Data: Ride history and payment details in Pi cryptocurrency.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide ride-sharing services, including matching riders with drivers.
  • Process payments securely using Pi cryptocurrency.
  • Improve our services through analytics and feedback.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share your information with:

  • Drivers, to facilitate ride coordination.
  • Third-party service providers for payment processing and analytics, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption and secure authentication to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at support@letride.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions, please email us at support@letride.com.
